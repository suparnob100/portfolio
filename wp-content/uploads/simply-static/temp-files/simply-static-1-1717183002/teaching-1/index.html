    Teaching

    My teaching philosophy prioritizes creating an optimal environment for student learning. I focus on fostering a space where ideas are freely shared and discussed, engaging students to enhance their understanding and retention.

    I emphasize drawing connections between concepts. For example, in dynamics classes, I revisit problems to demonstrate new concepts, helping students see the relationships between different principles. This approach, often not found in textbooks, is crucial for a comprehensive understanding.

    I aim to help students understand the types of problems they can solve with the concepts learned. When students approach me with issues, I guide them through fundamental concepts rather than providing direct solutions. This method helps them tackle similar problems independently in the future.
